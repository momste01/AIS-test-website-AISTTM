Soon to Arrive: Frinsa Coffee Across the Seas

September 2, 2024

In the serene highlands of Pangalengan, West Java, coffee is more than a crop. It is a living tradition. FRINSA Coffee, a name synonymous with nuance and care, has become a symbol of connection, culture, and craft. And soon, its story will continue in Vancouver.

A Tradition Rooted in People

At the heart of FRINSA are the farmers, who see themselves not merely as cultivators, but as stewards of heritage. Each cherry is hand picked at peak ripeness, then processed in facilities designed to elevate the distinct terroir of Pangalengan. Through washed, natural, honey, and experimental methods, FRINSA brings out flavor profiles ranging from citrus brightness to deep chocolate richness.

A Philosophy Beyond Processing

Yet what sets FRINSA apart is not only its precision, but its philosophy. The company invests in sustainable practices and empowers local farmers, ensuring that the land and the community flourish together. Each cup carries both a taste and a story, one of care, transparency, and culture.

Bringing FRINSA to Canada

Now, Across Indonesian Seas is proud to bring this story to Canada. The arrival of FRINSA Coffee in Vancouver marks the beginning of a new chapter, where the flavors of West Java connect with roasters, cafés, and coffee lovers here on the Pacific coast.

An Invitation in Every Cup

Every cup of FRINSA is an invitation to experience Indonesia's richness, bridging cultures one sip at a time.
