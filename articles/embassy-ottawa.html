AIS Visits the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia in Ottawa

March 31, 2026

On behalf of the entire Across Indonesian Seas team, we want to extend our deepest gratitude to the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia in Ottawa for welcoming us with such warmth and generosity.

The Visit

Our team members Akhtar and Morgan made the trip to Ottawa to meet with Ambassador Pak Muhsin and Commercial Attache Ibu Silky, along with the rest of the dedicated team at the Embassy. What followed was a conversation that reaffirmed everything we believe about the future of Canadian-Indonesian trade.

Together, we discussed what growing this trade relationship truly means: not just in terms of volume or market access, but in terms of the people on both sides. From the farmers of Indonesia to the businesses and consumers of Canada, the opportunity to build something that genuinely improves lives was at the center of every discussion.

Mapping Real Pathways

The meeting was not just about ideas. It was about action. Together with the Embassy team, we mapped out real, concrete pathways for collaboration. We explored how strengthening this trade corridor creates opportunity at every stage, from the farms and communities of Indonesia all the way to Canadian tables and cafes.

The Embassy's commitment to supporting Indonesian businesses and fostering meaningful economic ties with Canada was evident in every part of the conversation. Their guidance, insight, and enthusiasm gave us a clearer view of what is possible when institutions and private enterprise work hand in hand.

Thank You

To Ambassador Pak Muhsin, Ibu Silky, and the entire team at the Embassy: thank you. Thank you for your time, your vision, and your belief in what we are building. Your support means more to our team than we can express.

At Across Indonesian Seas, every relationship is the foundation of everything we do. This visit reminded us why. This is what trade looks like when it is done right.
