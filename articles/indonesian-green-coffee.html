Bringing the Richness of Indonesian Green Coffee to Canada

April 28, 2024

We are proud to announce our latest milestone: the direct purchase of specialty green coffee from Indonesia, now making its way to Canadian roasters. This shipment represents years of groundwork — relationship-building, sourcing visits, and the deliberate construction of a supply chain built on trust rather than convenience.

Why Indonesian Coffee

Indonesia is one of the world's most storied coffee origins, yet it remains underrepresented in the North American specialty market. Most of what reaches Canadian shelves is either Sumatra Mandheling processed through conventional channels, or anonymous commodity blends. West Java's highland coffees — dense, complex, and remarkably diverse in processing styles — have been largely invisible to Canadian roasters.

We saw that gap as an opportunity. Not just commercially, but as a chance to introduce roasters and their customers to flavors and stories that deserve to be known.

"West Java's highland coffees have been largely invisible to Canadian roasters. We saw that gap as an opportunity."

The Journey from Farm to Warehouse

Green coffee is a living material. From the moment it leaves the processing station, every decision in the supply chain affects what ends up in the roaster's hands. Our lots travel from Pangalengan in grain-pro-lined bags, staged at a licensed Indonesian exporter, then containerized through the Port of Tanjung Priok before crossing the Pacific to Vancouver.

Transit time is typically five to seven weeks. We test moisture and water activity at multiple points. On arrival, all lots are warehoused in climate-controlled storage and re-assessed by our team before being made available to roasters.

What's Available

Our inaugural shipment includes washed and natural lots from Java Frinsa Estate, in both full 60kg bag and partial quantities. We also carry a small selection of lots from Pusat Kopi Nusantara in Cianjur for roasters looking to explore the regional breadth of West Java's terroir.

To request a cupping sample or ask about current availability, use the contact form on our website. We respond to all roaster inquiries within one business day.
