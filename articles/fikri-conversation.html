From Farm to Future: A Conversation with Fikri from Frinsa Estate

June 9, 2024

At Across Indonesian Seas (AIS), we believe that every cup of coffee tells a story. Not just about flavor, but about people, place, and purpose. That is why we are proud to share our recent conversation with Fikri, our key partner at Frinsa Estate in West Java, Indonesia.

A Conversation from Origin

In our exclusive interview, Fikri offers a firsthand look at what makes Frinsa one of the most respected names in Indonesia's specialty coffee scene. From their unique processing methods to their commitment to community and sustainability, he walks us through how each batch of green coffee beans is crafted with care, consistency, and deep rooted passion.

More Than a Farm

Frinsa is more than just a farm. It is a movement. With meticulous attention to post harvest processing and a drive to continuously innovate, they have positioned themselves at the forefront of Indonesia's specialty coffee export market. Through our partnership with Frinsa, AIS is bringing premium, traceable green coffee to Canadian and North American roasters while also sharing the stories behind the beans. These are stories of resilience, tradition, and progress.

A Window into the Industry

This video is a window into that story. Hear directly from Fikri as he shares his insights into the evolving coffee industry, the challenges farmers face, and his hopes for the future of Indonesian coffee on the global stage.

Growing Together

We invite you to watch the full interview and learn more about the people behind the product. This collaboration is just the beginning, and we are honored to grow alongside partners like Frinsa.
