AIS and Frinsa Formalize Partnership to Bring Indonesian Coffee to Canada

May 12, 2024

Across Indonesian Seas (AIS) is proud to announce the formal signing of a Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) with Frinsa Estate, one of Indonesia's leading specialty coffee producers. This agreement lays the foundation for a strategic, long term collaboration aimed at bringing premium Indonesian green coffee to roasters and consumers across Canada.

Establishing the Partnership

Under this agreement, AIS will import and distribute green coffee sourced directly from Frinsa Estate's dry mill in West Java. The partnership reflects a shared commitment not only to trade, but to jointly manage quality control and operational standards. The goal is to ensure that every bag delivered meets the expectations of North American buyers.

Commitment to Quality and Transparency

This cooperation goes beyond commercial exchange. AIS and Frinsa have formally committed to maintaining consistent quality benchmarks, including moisture content standards, cupping scores, and grain safe packaging practices. Both parties will work closely on pre shipment coordination, origin side inspection, and transparent handover procedures. These steps are designed to ensure integrity, freshness, and traceability throughout the supply chain.

Roles and Execution

Frinsa Estate, known for its innovation in coffee processing and its focus on quality at origin, will begin fulfilling shipments under this agreement immediately. AIS will manage logistics, importation, and warehousing in Canada, ensuring that the coffee arrives fresh, consistent, and well documented.

Looking Ahead

This partnership represents a meaningful step forward in strengthening trade ties between Indonesian producers and Canadian markets. Both companies are committed to growing this collaboration in the years ahead.
